# Website Structure for ExtenSwitch

This document outlines the proposed structure for the official ExtenSwitch product website. The goal is to drive installations and build trust (especially for the Privacy Policy requirement).

## 1. Global Navigation (Header)
*   **Logo**: ExtenSwitch (Click to scroll to top)
*   **Links**: Features, How it Works, Privacy
*   **CTA Button**: "Add to Chrome" (Primary Action)

## 2. Hero Section (Above the Fold)
*   **Headline**: Switch Extensions in One Click. Browse Cleaner.
*   **Subheadline**: The fastest way to manage your Chrome extensions. Create profiles for work, crypto, or development and switch instantly.
*   **Primary CTA**: "Available in Chrome Web Store" (Button with Chrome Icon)
*   **Visual**: High-quality screenshot or mockups of the extension popup (showing the new Profiles UI).

## 3. "The Problem" Section
*   **Title**: Is your browser cluttered?
*   **Points**:
    *   Too many icons in the toolbar?
    *   Chrome using too much RAM?
    *   Need specific tools only for specific tasks?

## 4. Key Features
*   **Smart Profiles (New!)**: One-click switching between "Work", "Gaming", or "Crypto" setups. Save your snapshots.
*   **Instant Toggle**: Enable/disable extensions without digging into settings.
*   **Performance Boost**: Keep heavy extensions disabled until you actually need them.
*   **Privacy First**: No tracking, no analytics. Your data stays on your device.

## 5. How It Works
*   **Step 1**: Install from Chrome Web Store.
*   **Step 2**: Open the extension and disable unused tools.
*   **Step 3**: Save your perfect setup as a Profile.
*   **Step 4**: Switch modes anytime with one click.
